# Project Name

## Decision

The project name is:

```text
Mizan
```

Meaning: balance, scale, measure.

Why it fits:

- The core product is credit balance, fair limits, metering, and accountability.
- It is short, memorable, and not locked to only AI rental.
- It works well for open-source infrastructure.

Suggested repo/package names:

- `mizan`
- `mizan-gateway`
- `mizan-ai`

## Alternative Names Considered

### Neraca

Meaning: balance sheet, scale, measured account.

Why it fits:

- Very clear connection to credits, balances, and accountable usage.
- Strong Indonesian flavor.
- Feels practical without losing the balance/accounting meaning.

Possible package name:

- `neraca`
- `neraca-gateway`

### Pramana

Meaning: valid knowledge, proof, reliable means of knowing.

Why it fits:

- AI access needs trust, accounting, and auditability.
- Feels philosophical without being obscure.
- Good for a gateway that records usage truthfully.

Possible package name:

- `pramana`
- `pramana-gateway`

### Anvaya

Meaning: connection, continuity, relation.

Why it fits:

- A gateway connects users, keys, routes, models, and providers.
- Sounds calm and infrastructure-like.

Possible package name:

- `anvaya`
- `anvaya-router`

### Tula

Meaning: scale, balance, weighing.

Why it fits:

- Shorter and softer than Mizan.
- Good for a product centered on fair allocation and credit balance.
- Philosophical without sounding too enterprise.

Possible package name:

- `tula`
- `tula-ai`

### Nadi

Meaning: flow, pulse, channel.

Why it fits:

- Good metaphor for traffic flow through an AI gateway.
- Short and product-friendly.

Possible package name:

- `nadi`
- `nadi-ai`

### Setara

Meaning: equal, balanced, fair.

Why it fits:

- Good for a credit/fair-access gateway.
- Clear Indonesian identity.

Possible package name:

- `setara-ai`
- `setara-gateway`

### Dharana

Meaning: holding, sustaining, concentration.

Why it fits:

- Good for an infrastructure layer that holds access, policy, and continuity.
- Feels deep and philosophical.
- Better for a serious developer platform than a transactional product.

Possible package name:

- `dharana`
- `dharana-gateway`

### Aksara

Meaning: letter, syllable, the imperishable.

Why it fits:

- AI systems are text/token heavy, so the name has a natural language connection.
- Memorable in Indonesian.
- Philosophical but still approachable.

Possible package name:

- `aksara-ai`
- `aksara-gateway`

### Satyam

Meaning: truth.

Why it fits:

- Strong link to auditability, truthful metering, and reliable accounting.
- Feels principled and philosophical.

Possible package name:

- `satyam`
- `satyam-gateway`

## More Options

- `Marga`: path or route.
- `Sutra`: thread or connection.
- `Vahana`: vehicle or carrier.
- `Kirana`: ray or beam.
- `Takar`: measure or ration.
- `Jatah`: quota, but more casual.
- `Laras`: harmony or alignment.
- `Timbang`: weigh or measure, practical Indonesian.
- `Ukara`: expression or sentence.
- `Mantra`: invocation or phrase, memorable but a bit mystical.
- `Mandala`: ordered system or circle.
- `Rasa`: essence or feel, beautiful but less infrastructure-specific.
- `Sama`: equal or balanced.
- `Tirta`: flow/water, good for routing but less credit-specific.

## My Pick

For the open-source repo, the chosen name is:

```text
Mizan
```

It keeps the core identity philosophical and infrastructure-friendly.

If you want alternatives beyond Mizan, my strongest three are:

```text
Neraca
Pramana
Tula
```

`Neraca` is the clearest Indonesian option for credit/balance. `Pramana` is the
most philosophical and trustworthy. `Tula` is the shortest and cleanest brand.
