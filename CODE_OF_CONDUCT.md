# Code of Conduct

Mizan is an open-source infrastructure project. We want the project space to be
useful, respectful, and welcoming for people who are learning, maintaining, or
deploying the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially during technical disagreement.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the people affected by decisions.
- Give clear feedback without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy, credentials, and security-sensitive reports.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Dismissing security concerns without review.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions.

## Reporting

If you need to report behavior or a security-sensitive concern, use a private
maintainer channel when available. Do not publish exploit details, credentials,
or private user data in public issues.

Maintainers may moderate comments, lock threads, or block participants when
needed to protect the project and its contributors.
